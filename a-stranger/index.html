A Stranger

Zel December 12, 2025

I was distracted, going home on 71,

and you turned your head left, waiting for cars to move through

my lane

You were struck by the winter sun—

your face, Caravaggio, held in my frame

a brief intent carried in the crossing of shadow and light

Were you reticent? Were you pure?

A stranger, a window, a mirror, a feeling
